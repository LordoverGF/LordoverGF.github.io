About PT IJS

Mojong was founded in December 1970 by Mr. Albert Likadinata. Initially operating in the distribution service sector, Mojong now has its headquarters and 12 branches across West, Southeast, and South Sulawesi provinces.

Our expansion into Papua began in 1980 when Mr. Likadinata opened a branch in Sorong. In 1990, we acquired PT Irian Jaya Sehat in Jayapura, continuing our expansion to cover most regencies and provinces in Papua.

Today, PT Irian Jaya Sehat operates in the distribution service sector, providing a wide range of FMCG products, including food and beverages, rice and cooking oil, beauty and personal care products, and household items. We collaborate with leading suppliers to ensure the high quality and authenticity of the products we distribute. Over our 43 years in Papua, the company has grown rapidly, expanding its business reach to 12 branches across the region.
